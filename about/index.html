About Me

Jeffery Guo is from Eden Prairie, MN and is currently a senior studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, expected to graduate May 2024.

Jeffery intends to pursue a PhD in Mechanical Engineering and is interested in research on miniaturized medical robots and devices to help diagnose and treat various diseases.

In his free time, Jeffery enjoys playing piano, violin, and guitar, producing music, working out at the gym, and playing and watching soccer.
